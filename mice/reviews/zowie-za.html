Zowie ZA Reviews

ESEA User "abx"
"The Zowie ZA12 is perfect for "switch shooters" (similar to switch hitters in Baseball) who either want a palm or a claw grip at any point in time.Combine that with the usual Zowie perks such as being completely driverless as well as being ambidextrous and you have yourself a great mouse, if not the best mouse ever made.Only con would be the Zowie switches which are pretty notorious for being insanely stiff on the click, especially if you're used to the light switches used on most other "gaming" mice. Recommend the ZA11 if you have a hand larger than that of an infants (unlike myself). Or the ZA13 if you're on the fetus level of hand size."
